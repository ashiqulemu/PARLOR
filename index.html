Welcome to The Parlor

The Parlor at Schenectady Municipal Golf Course is open to the public year round offering high quality food and beverages in our beautifully restored historic facility, and on the golf course itself.

Whether you're looking for something to snack on after playing a round of golf, having a great meal in a beautiful restaurant overlooking the golf course, or hosting your special event, The Parlor is your place to do it all!
